the crew
what we stand for

A vanilla Minecraft SMP where adult creators find a genuine home — a trusted space to build, create, and grow together.

Communication Creativity Maturity Respect Community Commitment Fair Play
ᚦ Communication
If something is wrong, say it. If you need a break, post it in the leave-of-absence channel. No silent exits, no dropped DMs. Transparency keeps the community tight.
ᚲ Creativity & Craftsmanship
Whether it is a massive build, a clever redstone contraption, or a well-timed prank — we take pride in what we make and celebrate what others make too.
ᛗ Maturity Without Seriousness
We are a grown-up server, but that does not mean we are boring. We can be silly, competitive, and chaotic — and still handle conflict like adults when it matters.
ᛊ Respect
Treat every member with kindness. No bullying, harassment, or discrimination. Some members may not wish to engage in PVP or pranks — and that is okay.
ᚹ Community Over Content
Yes, we are all content creators. But the server comes before the algorithm. We show up for each other, not just for the upload schedule.
ᛏ Commitment to the Season
Joining a season means committing to the journey. Not every session has to be groundbreaking — but showing up and being part of the world we are building together is what makes this worth it.
ᛟ Fair Play
Vanilla. No gameplay-altering mods or datapacks — only quality-of-life additions already in place. The playing field is level. The outcomes are not.
